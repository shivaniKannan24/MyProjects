Mineral and Natural Fertilizers

  • Rock Phosphate:

    This natural mineral fertilizer contains phosphorus and various trace elements. It's a slow-release fertilizer used to improve root development and flowering in plants.

  • Potassium Sulfate:

    It is a mineral fertilizer providing potassium and sulfur, vital for plant growth, root development, and disease resistance.

  • Dolomite:

    This is a natural source of calcium and magnesium carbonate. It adjusts soil pH levels and supplies calcium and magnesium nutrients.

  • Gypsum:

    It is a mineral containing calcium and sulfur. Gypsum improves soil structure, aids in water penetration, and helps plants absorb nutrients.

  • Greensand:

    Derived from marine deposits, greensand is rich in potassium, iron, and other minerals. It enhances soil structure and improves moisture retention.

  • Bone Meal:

    This organic-mineral fertilizer provides phosphorus and calcium, promoting root development and strong flowering in plants.

  • Blood Meal:

    It is a nitrogen-rich natural fertilizer made from dried animal blood. It's a quick-release nitrogen source used to stimulate green growth.

  • Fish Emulsion:

    Made from processed fish, this organic fertilizer is high in nitrogen and provides a quick nutrient boost to plants.

  • Seaweed Extract:

    Derived from seaweed, it contains various micronutrients, growth hormones, and trace elements that stimulate plant growth and improve stress resistance.

  • Epsom Salt (Magnesium Sulfate):

    It's a mineral supplement that provides magnesium and sulfur to plants, promoting chlorophyll production and overall growth.
